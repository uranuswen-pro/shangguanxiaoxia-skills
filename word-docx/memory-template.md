## Common Templates

- Font: Times New Roman

- Line spacing: 1.5
- Margins: 2.5cm left/right, 2cm top/bottom

### Chinese Document
- Font: SimSun (宋体) for body
- Font: SimHei (黑体) for headings
- Font size: 12pt
